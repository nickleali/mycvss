## Abstract

This session will cover updates of the CVSS SIG efforts to date, discuss the new CVSS Consumer Implementation Guide, the state of CVSS v4.0, and the future of CVSS.




## Abstract for Program Agenda

During this talk, Nick will cover the ongoing business of the FIRST CVSS SIG. Topics include document updates from the CVSS SIG over the past year; publication of the CVSS Consumer Implementation Guide; and the in progress revision of CVSS v4.0.

Please bring your questions and requests for examples to discuss.

Opportunity for feedback for the future of CVSS.
